SalesPilot Application User Guide

Welcome to the SalesPilot user guide. This application is designed to manage products and services, its sales, inventory, and analytics with a user-friendly menu layout. Use the sections below to understand each feature and menu of SalesPilot.

Product Management

To add a new product, follow these steps:

  • Enter the product details, including its name, category, unit price, and other details in the form.
  • Once a product is added, its Category and prices should be re-updated when the page refreshes. Then click the update button to display the latest details.
  • Products can only be edited in the Product Menu only.
  • Once a product is added, its Category and Prices should be re-updated after the page refreshes, then click the update button to insert and display the latest details.
Sales Management

To record sales transactions, follow these steps:

  • Enter the product name as it is written in the product records, then enter the unit price, sales quantity, total price, and other sales data. Only existing products can be inserted into sale records, which can be managed through the Add Sale page.
  • Submit the sales data. This data will update in the Sales and Inventory sections, ensuring inventory levels reflect the sales.
Data Categories: Adding and Viewing

In addition to products and sales, you can manage other key data categories:

  • Category: Access the Category Menu to view products grouped by category for easier management.
  • Inventory: View all sales transactions and stock levels within the Inventory Menu.
  • People: Manage Customers, Staff, and Suppliers through the People Menu, where you can add or update contact and profile information as needed.
  • Expenses: Record expenses related to sales or inventory operations in the Expenses page. This information will be used for financial analytics.
  • Invoices: Manage and view invoices on the Invoices page. This information will be used for financial tracking and reporting.
Analytical and Performance Reporting

To track key performance indicators, the following sections provide analytical insights and reporting:

  • Charts and Reports Menu: Access real-time charts and detailed reports, showing overall analytics on product performance, revenue, and profitability.
  • Performance Analysis:
    • Product-Based Sales Metrics: View product metrics for individual items on the Inventory Metrics page to help identify top-performing products.
    • Analytics Report: View available stock, sell-through rates, and inventory turnover for each product on the Analytics Report page.
    • Category-Based Sales Metrics: Analyze sales by product category on the Sales Metrics page to assess which categories are contributing most to overall sales.
    • Supply: Monitor product and service deliveries and purchases through the Supply Menu using the invoices and suppliers details.
